# Vocabulary Tracker

## Target Audience
**Beginner-Intermediate Korean learners** starting a particle physics research story.

Students should have basic Korean knowledge before starting Chapter 1. This includes:
- Basic greetings and conversation patterns
- Personal pronouns and demonstratives
- Common particles and connectors
- Essential everyday verbs and adjectives
- Basic number system and time telling
- Common computer/office vocabulary

---

## Notes for Validation

When validating chapters:

1. **Baseline words** from the Prerequisites section should NOT be flagged as errors
2. **Domain-specific vocabulary** should be introduced gradually through chapters
3. **Grammar patterns** must follow the progression plan strictly
4. **Technical terms** (CMS, ROOT, CERN, etc.) can appear without formal introduction
5. **Character knowledge** should be appropriate for their introduction chapter
6. **Time expressions** can use numbers from the baseline prerequisites

### Vocabulary Growth Strategy:
- Chapters 1-4 establish the **research lab environment** vocabulary
- Future chapters will add: experimental procedures, data analysis terms, physics concepts
- Keep new words per chapter to 7-10 maximum
- Reinforce previous vocabulary frequently
